## Mindset - 1.0 vs 2.0

**1.0**  

* Task oriented
* Do as directed

**2.0**  

* Problem solvers
* Understands business
* Provides valuable inputs
* Excellent soft skills
  * Communication
  * Expectations management
  * Stake holders management
  * Meeting deadlines
  * Computational thinking approach
* Goes extra mile
